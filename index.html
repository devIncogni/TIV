The Bangladeshi Shikkarthi

Our first international chapter - The Bangladeshi Shikkarthi ensures that Bangladeshi students get the best of resources that they need to be successful in their careers and fulfill their aspirations. Ensuring that every student has equal success opportunities, to soar high in life is of utmost importance. In collaboration with the University of Rajshahi, it successfully organised a workshop on Study Abroad with 250+ students featuring notable speakers from Stanford University, Kings College London and University of Warwick. With a vision to host even bigger sessions in the future, it is looking forward to hold offline sessions in the best universities in Bangladesh. Its expansion in just a matter of days is a testament to its impactful work.

Our Events

Young Minds Changemakers Summit 2024

One of the collaborators for YOUNG MINDS Changemakers Summit 2024 by YOUNG MINDS. Held from December 13th to 15th, 2024, at O.P. Jindal Global University, this inspiring summit brought together 50 youth-led social impact organizations. Over three days, participants collaborated with experts in workshops, shared innovative solutions, and networked with changemakers. Cultural evenings celebrated India's diversity, making this summit a true catalyst for young leaders driving meaningful change.

Earth Dialogue 2024

Earth Dialogue 2024 empowered youth to take action for a sustainable future. Through expert-led workshops and hands-on sessions, participants gained practical tools to address environmental challenges. This program inspired young leaders to innovate and act, paving the way for impactful, climate-focused solutions.
